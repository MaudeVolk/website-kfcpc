Mike Wilkinson has served as the pastor of Knoxville First Cumberland Presbyterian Church since September 2013. Originally, from Knoxville and a graduate of Karns High School, he entered in the ministry in 1992 after a twelve-year career in civil engineering and construction. Before he came back to Knoxville, he pastored three other congregations, one in West Tennessee and two in Alabama. He earned his Master of Divinity degree from Memphis Theological Seminary; he also holds degrees from Roane State Community College and Bethel College. He has been active on the denominational level and currently serves as the president of the Ministry Council of the Cumberland Presbyterian Church. Pastor Mike has been married to his gracious wife, Joanna, since 1982. They have three daughters, two son-in-laws, and three awesome grandchildren with two additional due to be born this year.
