Sam and Karen Ware are the Music Directors of Knoxville First Cumberland Presbyterian Church. Sam is the Music Director/Organist, and Karen is the Choral Director.  They are both graduates of Tennessee Wesleyan College in Athens, TN and have been leading church music since 1970. Sam and Karen have been at Knoxville First Cumberland Presbyterian since 2005. ^p The Wares have three daughters and two granddaughters. Karen is a Realtor and is the Office Administrator of Century 21, Legacy. Sam is Senior Funeral Director at Rose Mortuary Mann Heritage Chapel.
